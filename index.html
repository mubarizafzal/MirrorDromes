This page finds MirrorDromes in a given input both recursively and iteratively.

What MirrorDrome() does is that it takes a sentence as its input, and searches for any sub-sections of that sentence that is mirrored from its middle. This function also takes into consideration letters that are mirrored themselves, those being: 'i', 'l', 'm', 'n', 'o, 't', 'u', 'v', 'w', and 'x'. As well as two letters that are can be mirrored down the middle when side-by-side, those being: 'db' and 'bd', 'qp' and 'pq', and also 'sz' and 'zs'.

For example, when the given sentence is "commodore".
The output should be: o,ommo,o,mm,m,m,o
